ITALIAN NAVY / ITALIAN ARMY
Jan 2011 – Aug 2013 · 2 years 8 months
Italy

In the defence sector, I designed and coordinated digital solutions to support document management, information security, and the integration of administrative and operational processes. For the Italian Army between 2004 and 2005, and for the Italian Navy between 2011 and 2013, I contributed to the implementation of document dematerialisation solutions compliant with both civil and military regulations, overseeing the entire document lifecycle: acquisition, classification, registration, electronic signature, semantic annotation of metadata, compliant archiving, and full traceability. The initiative involved both central structures and distributed units, with an approach aimed at ensuring operational sustainability, administrative continuity, and regulatory oversight of the information processed.

Competenze: Gestione dei documenti · Archiviazione digitale · Knowledge management · Business Process Management

SICILIAN REGIONAL GOVERNMENT
Jul 1999 – Sep 2010 · 11 years 3 months

I supported the Sicilian Regional Government in adopting open-source technologies with high economic sustainability, achieving significant savings by replacing proprietary software with free solutions and reusing existing hardware. As an expert in the PAFlow document management platform, I led the introduction of interoperable tools compliant with current regulations, training managers and civil servants in digital registration, electronic signature, long-term archiving, and document classification. This approach anticipated by nearly ten years the model of the “digital citizen dossier” (and the IO app — the application for interoperability of Italian public services).

Competenze: Linux · Project Management · Knowledge management · Software open source · Gestione dei documenti · SDLC
